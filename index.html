About IEEE

⚡

IEEE Global

The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) is the world's largest technical professional organization, dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity. With over 400,000 members in 160+ countries, IEEE shapes the future of engineering and technology.

Visit IEEE.org →
🎓

IEEE SB LBSITW

The IEEE Student Branch at LBS Institute of Technology for Women is a vibrant community of passionate student engineers. We foster technical excellence, professional growth, and collaborative innovation through workshops, hackathons, and networking events throughout the year.

Instagram LinkedIn IEEE.org
🔥

IEEE Ignite

IEEE Ignite is our flagship technical event, conceived to bridge the gap between classroom learning and industry-ready skills. Through curated workshops, professional development sessions, and competitive challenges, we ignite the potential within every student.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

IEEE Ignite is open to all students from any engineering college or university. Both IEEE members and non-IEEE members are welcome to register and participate. However, IEEE members may receive priority registration and discounted fees.

Click the "Register Now" button in the navigation bar or at the bottom of the page. Fill in the registration form with your details, select your preferred events, and complete the payment. You'll receive a confirmation email with your delegate pass shortly after.

Yes! You can register for multiple events as long as they are not scheduled at the same time. Please check the schedule carefully before registering. Some events may have limited seats, so early registration is recommended.

Registration fees are as follows: IEEE Members — ₹199 per event or ₹499 for the full pass. Non-IEEE Members — ₹249 per event or ₹649 for the full pass. Early bird discounts are available until July 10, 2025. Team events may have separate pricing.

Yes! All participants who attend the events will receive digital participation certificates. Winners and runners-up of competitive events will receive special merit certificates along with prizes. Certificates will be distributed at the valedictory ceremony and also sent via email.

Limited accommodation facilities are available on campus for outstation participants on a first-come, first-served basis. Please indicate your requirement during registration. Additional accommodation options are available in nearby hotels. Contact us at ieee.sb.lbsitw@gmail.com for assistance.

Bring your college ID card, registration confirmation (printed or digital), a laptop (for technical workshops), notebook and pen, and any event-specific materials mentioned in your confirmation email. Dress code is smart casual. Food and refreshments will be provided.

You can reach us via email at ieee.sb.lbsitw@gmail.com, or through our Instagram and LinkedIn pages (@ieee_sb_lbsitw). For urgent queries, WhatsApp support will be active closer to the event dates. We typically respond within 24 hours.
